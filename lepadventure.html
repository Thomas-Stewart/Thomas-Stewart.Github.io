  • This simple Android application follows the journey of a young hero leprechaun as he quests for his pot of gold. It's a choose-your-own-adventure with an Irish twist.

    Available on the Google Play Store as "Leprechaun Adventure"

  • Choose the destiny of this fantastic little Irishman as he traverses the area for his pot o' gold. There are many decisions in his future, and only you can ensure his mission is a success!

    This was both my second experience with Android development. I learned a lot about how views are used in Android Studio and how intents are used to push data into outside activities.
